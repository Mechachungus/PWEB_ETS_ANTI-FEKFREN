Your adventure begins here

Ready for a sun-drenched escape? Sitting at the heart of the mountains, our hotel is a lovingly developed Finca, blending rustic charm with modern luxury for an intimate, 27-bedroom experience.

Staying at Eastout

Choose from our 27 beautifully designed rooms, each with its own character. From the unique Tower Suites to rooms with private terraces boasting soaring sunrise views, you'll find the perfect, comfortable space to relax after a day of exploring the island.

Dining

Savour authentic local dishes crafted from fresh produce grown in our own gardens and orchards, perfectly paired with award-winning local wines. Our chefs bring vibrant, sun-ripened flavours to life at our terrace restaurant, where Mediterranean cuisine is served alongside breathtaking views.

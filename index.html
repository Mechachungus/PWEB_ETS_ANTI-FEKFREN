Discover Eastout Hotel

Mallorca's most exciting hotel

Ready for a Mediterranean escape? Sitting at the heart of the estate is the 18th-century Finca - or manor house - which has been lovingly developed into the intimate, luxurious 27-bedroom Son Bunyola Hotel.

Staying at Eastout

Choose from the hotel's 27 beautifully designed rooms, each with its own character and charm. From the truly unique Tower Suites built in 13th and 19th century turrets, to suites with private terraces boasting soaring views and luxurious rooms with the comfiest of beds, perfect for relaxing after a day exploring the island.

Dining

At Eastout, savour authentic Mallorcan dishes crafted from fresh produce grown in the estate's gardens and orchards, perfectly paired with award-winning local wines carefully selected by our sommelier. Our chefs bring these vibrant flavours to life at Sa Terrassa, where Mediterranean cuisine is served alongside breathtaking sea views.
